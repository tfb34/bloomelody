Meet the

Team

PBC Labs is a diverse team of designers, developers, and engineers interested in creating human-centered design products that help consumers lead happier and healthier lives. As a team with family in the field, we have a personal stake in delivering a quality solution for therapists that work with children with developmental delays. Our combined experience with the help of our clinical consultants makes us the ideal team to conceptualize, design, develop, and implement in your classroom and beyond.
